A human-in-the-loop clinical orchestration platform — tracking risk in real time, holding staff to response SLAs, and asking a person before it ever moves a patient.

A rapidly deteriorating patient

Six steps, one patient — John Doe, 65, admitted with breathing difficulty.

01
Nurse Sarahpatient admission
John is admitted to the general ward
action

Sarah opens the dashboard and admits John into a general ward bed.

system

The admission and initial vitals save instantly, and every active dashboard in the hospital updates over WebSockets — no refresh needed.

02
Nurse Sarahvitals monitoring
Two hours in, his numbers turn
action

SpO2 falls to 84%, heart rate climbs to 135 bpm. Sarah logs both.

system

The NEWS2 scoring engine recalculates instantly — risk score jumps to 9, status flips from stable to critical.

03
The systemSLA alert generation
The clock starts
action

The alerts engine fires a CRITICAL_VITALS alert the instant the score crosses the threshold.

system

A strict 5-minute SLA attaches to the alert. Every doctor on shift gets it in their notification tray.

04
Dr. Chenresponse center
Someone has to answer before the timer does
action

Dr. Chen sees the alert flashing on the Kanban board and clicks Acknowledge with time to spare.

system

The SLA timer stops. Had it lapsed, a background escalation worker — running every 60 seconds — would have pushed this straight to the Chief Medical Officer.

05
The OrchestratorAI recommendation
The AI proposes a move — it can't make one
action

Now that John is critical, the Orchestrator scans live bed telemetry across the hospital.

system

It finds ICU Bed 12 open and generates a relocation recommendation, with its clinical reasoning attached — SpO2 below 85% needs ventilation.

06
Dr. Chenhuman-in-the-loop approval
A person signs off, and only then does anything move
action

The recommendation sits pending on the Action Center. Dr. Chen reviews the reasoning and clicks Approve.

system

The database transaction runs — John is reassigned to the ICU bed, and hospital-wide capacity updates live for everyone watching.
